About Us

 J & V
Resort & Hotel

Established in 2021, J&V Hotel and Resort is owned and managed by Janpac Realty and Development, registered under Securities Exchange Commission (SEC) with the business name, Janpac Group of Companies.

Relax in your choice of our guest rooms and suites at J&V Hotel and Resort, each designed to meet the needs of both business and leisure travelers with a range of convenient amenities and services.

Function Hall

Our Function Hall can cater all kinds of momentous events. We have the perfect place for Weddings, birthdays, christening, seminars, reunions and many more.

Restaurant

Explore local and international cuisine in vibrant all-you-can-eat buffets and from a la carte menus. Relax in serene surroundings with views of the pool through floor-to-ceiling windows and enjoy an array of fresh and healthy dishes from around the world.
